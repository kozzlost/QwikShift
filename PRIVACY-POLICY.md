# QwikShift Privacy Policy

## 1. Introduction

QwikShift ("Company," "we," "us," "our," or "ourselves") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our mobile application and website (collectively, the "Service").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Service. By accessing and using QwikShift, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by all the provisions of this Privacy Policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information You Provide
- **Account Registration**: Name, email address, phone number, date of birth, government-issued ID (drivers license, passport)
- - **Payment Information**: Credit card numbers, banking information (processed securely via Stripe - we do not store full card details)
  - - **Profile Information**: Professional experience, certifications, background check authorization
    - - **Location Data**: Current location and work location preferences
      - - **Communication**: Messages between workers and employers, support inquiries
       
        - ### 2.2 Information Automatically Collected
        - - **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, mobile network information, device identifier
          - - **Usage Data**: Pages visited, time spent, features accessed, clicks, search queries
            - - **Location Services**: Precise GPS coordinates (with your explicit permission in-app)
              - - **Analytics**: Google Analytics, Mixpanel event tracking
               
                - ### 2.3 Information From Third Parties
                - - **Background Check Providers**: Criminal history reports (via third-party verification services)
                  - - **Identity Verification**: Government ID verification results
                    - - **Social Media**: LinkedIn or other social platform data (if you choose to connect)

                                                                                                - ## 5. Data Security
                                                                                               
                                                                                                - ### 5.1 Encryption
                                                                                                - - **In Transit**: All data transmitted via HTTPS/TLS 1.3 encryption
                                                                                                  - - **At Rest**: Sensitive data encrypted using AES-256-GCM
                                                                                                    - - **Passwords**: Bcrypt hashing with salt
                                                                                                      - - **API Keys**: Encrypted and never stored in plaintext
                                                                                                       
                                                                                                        - ### 5.2 Security Measures
                                                                                                        - - Multi-factor authentication (SMS & Email 2FA, TOTP)
                                                                                                          - - Regular security audits and penetration testing
                                                                                                            - - Rate limiting on APIs (prevent brute force attacks)
                                                                                                              - - Input validation and sanitization (prevent XSS, SQL injection)
                                                                                                                - - Content Security Policy headers
                                                                                                                  - - CORS restrictions on API endpoints
                                                                                                                   
                                                                                                                    - ### 5.3 Data Retention
                                                                                                                    - - **Account Data**: Retained as long as your account is active
                                                                                                                      - - **Transaction History**: Retained for 7 years (tax/legal compliance)
                                                                                                                        - - **Deleted Account Data**: Permanently deleted within 90 days of account deletion (except legally required records)
                                                                                                                          - - **Support Communications**: Retained for 2 years
                                                                                                                          - **Analytics Data**: Aggregated data retained indefinitely; individual-level data deleted after 24 months
                                                                                                                         
                                                                                                                          - ### 5.4 Security Incidents
                                                                                                                          - In the event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will:
                                                                                                                          - - Notify affected users within 30 days
                                                                                                                            - - Notify law enforcement and regulators as required
                                                                                                                            - Provide free credit monitoring services
                                                                                                                            - - Publish a post-incident security report
